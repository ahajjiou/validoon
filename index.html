Validoon

Instant clarity for risky inputs. Validoon helps you quickly triage anything you copy/paste (URLs, text snippets, logs, tokens) and label it as ALLOW, WARN, or BLOCK — with short reasons you can act on.

Why it works: it runs fully in your browser (no uploads, no server calls), so you get fast results without exposing sensitive data.
